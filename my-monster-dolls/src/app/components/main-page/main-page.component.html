Monster High is an American fashion doll franchise created by Mattel and launched on June 11, 2010. The characters are inspired by monster movies, sci-fi horror, thriller fiction, and various other creatures. Monster High was created by Garrett Sander, with illustrations by Kellee Riley and illustrator Glen Hanson.
On February 23, 2021, Mattel announced the return of the Monster High brand, promising new content and products for the following year. Its television division announced a new animated series and a live-action film based on the franchise. Both projects will air on Nickelodeon in the United States in 2022.
